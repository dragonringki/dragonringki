About Me

Matthew

DragonRingki

Hi, I'm Matthew, a cybersecurity enthusiast based in the UK. I'm currently exploring the fascinating world of hacking and security through Hack The Box challenges and online learning resources.

My Journey

My passion for technology began with my first encounter with Windows 95 on our family computer. Since then, I've been captivated by the intricacies of computer systems and the ever-evolving landscape of digital threats. I'm particularly interested in the art of social engineering and the power of human psychology in exploiting vulnerabilities.

Skills and Experience

My main strengths are social engineering and scripting. I've gained hands-on experience through Hack The Box, but I'm looking for ethical real-world experience. I'm also actively working towards my CompTIA A+ certification.

Goals and Aspirations

My goal is to become a skilled security assessor (pentester), helping individuals and organizations protect their digital assets. I'm passionate about educating others about cybersecurity risks and empowering them to take proactive measures to safeguard their information.

Outside of Cybersecurity

While the digital world is my playground, I believe in finding balance. When I'm not immersed in cybersecurity challenges, I'm often found knee-deep in a river, fly rod in hand. Fly fishing is my escape, a way to reconnect with nature and unwind from the online world.

As a dedicated family man, I also enjoy spending time with my loved ones. Setting up Minecraft servers for my kids has become a shared passion, allowing us to bond over building virtual worlds and embarking on exciting adventures together. It's a reminder that while technology can be a powerful tool, it's the connections we make with others that truly matter.
